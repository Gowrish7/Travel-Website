Bangalore

Bangalore also called as Silicon Valley of India. It is not only a place to work but even has a rich culture and tasty foods.The Bangalore Palace which was built long back is a place that anyone must visit.
Bangalore also has other cool things to see, like old temples and museums, and places to eat.

Hyderabad

A city known for its rich history, mouth-watering cuisine, especially biryani and bustling bazaars.
Located in the southern part of India, Hyderabad is a destination that will take you on a journey through the past and present.
